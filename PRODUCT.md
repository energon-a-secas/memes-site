# Product

## Register

product

## Users

People on a team (and adjacent internet folks) who want a shared, searchable stash of memes and internal jokes: Simpsons classics, anime references, reaction images. They come to browse, grab a meme to drop in chat, vote on the good ones, and occasionally upload their own (named or anonymous).

## Product Purpose

Meme Vault is a browse-vote-upload archive of memes organized by category. The job: find the right meme fast (search, category filter, random button), react to it (upvote/downvote), download it, or contribute one. Success is a quick "found it, grabbed it" loop, with light community signal (votes) and zero-friction browsing.

## Brand Personality

Neorgon voice with the volume turned toward fun: the subtitle literally counts "N internal jokes." Casual, irreverent, in-group. Three words: capable, generous, fun. Never tries to be a serious content platform; it's a shared joke drawer.

## Anti-references

All suite anti-references, plus: ad-choked meme aggregator sites, engagement-bait infinite feeds, and forced-login walls before you can even look. Browsing and downloading never require an account.

## Design Principles

1. **Browse without a login.** Search, filter, random, and download all work anonymous. Auth gates only personal contribution attribution and vote identity.
2. **Fast grid, fast grab.** A responsive image grid is the whole product; everything else is chrome.
3. **Light community signal.** Votes add color, not pressure; no leaderboards, no streaks.
4. **Anonymous-friendly upload.** Contributors can post named or anonymous.
5. Inherits all root suite principles.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG 2.2 AA targets from the suite. Vote buttons and nav controls carry labels; vote score is conveyed by text + color, never color alone. Honors `prefers-reduced-motion` for the `voteBurst` and grid-load animations.
